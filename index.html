Why is my Beam B6 showing 91% utilisation? Should I be worried?
✦
91% means the beam uses 91% of its allowable capacity per IS 456 Annex G. It's technically a pass — but B6 is a transfer beam carrying load from a discontinued column, so I'd recommend keeping utilisation below 80% for safety margin.
